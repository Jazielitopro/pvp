# Combat System

## Weapon Type
Projectile-based system.

## Base Weapon (MVP)
- Revolver
- 6 bullets
- 3 hits to kill
- High damage per shot
- Requires reload

## Combat Rhythm
- Peek → Shoot → Reposition
- Not sustained fire
- High punishment for missing shots

## Damage Model
- Chunk-based damage (not DPS)
- No random spread (or very minimal)
- Precision-based

## Health System
- Fixed max health
- Passive regeneration after delay
- Encourages disengage and reposition

## Design Goals
- Every shot matters
- Every mistake is punishable
- Combat is readable and fair

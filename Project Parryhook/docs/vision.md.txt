# Vision

## High Concept
A fast-paced competitive PvP shooter in Roblox focused on movement, precision, and timing.

Players engage in intense duels using:
- Advanced movement
- Projectile weapons
- Tactical positioning
- Future parry mechanics

## Player Fantasy
The player feels like:
- Fast
- Precise
- Untouchable when skilled
- Outplaying opponents through mechanics

## Pillars
- Speed with control
- Skill expression
- Competitive fairness
- Replayability
- Mechanical mastery

## What Makes This Game Unique
- Projectile combat in a high-speed environment
- Grapple-based vertical combat
- Parry system (future core mechanic)
- Trade-off perk system
- First-person precision gameplay

## Target Experience
- Fast but readable
- Punishing but fair
- High skill ceiling
- Highly replayable

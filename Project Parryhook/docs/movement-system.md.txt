# Movement System

## Core Mechanics
- Walk / Sprint
- Dash
- Slide
- Jump
- Air control

## Dash Design
- No invulnerability frames
- Used for repositioning only
- High skill expression tool

## Movement Philosophy
Movement is:
- Offensive
- Defensive
- Strategic

Not just traversal.

## Constraints
- Must remain readable to other players
- Must not break hit registration
- Must feel responsive locally

## Risks
- Too fast = desync
- Too slow = boring

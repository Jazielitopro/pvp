# Progression System

## Goals
- Reward player engagement
- Encourage continued play
- Provide cosmetic customization
- Avoid pay-to-win mechanics

## Core Progression
- XP per match
- Account level
- Unlock rewards over time

## Rewards
- Weapon skins
- Grapple effects
- Dash trails
- Parry effects (future)
- Player cosmetics

## Currency
- Soft currency (earned in matches)
- Optional premium currency

## Loot Systems
- Loot boxes / drops
- Random rewards with rarity tiers
- Pity system recommended

## Competitive Integrity Rule
Progression must NOT affect:
- Damage
- Health
- Core gameplay advantage

## Perks Consideration
- Perks are gameplay affecting
- Must be balanced for ranked
- May require:
  - full unlock in ranked
  - or restricted usage

## Long-Term Systems (Future)
- Battle pass
- Seasonal rewards
- Challenges
